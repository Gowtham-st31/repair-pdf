Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Overview

Repair PDF Ninja is a free online PDF editor that helps users edit, merge, reorder and repair PDF files. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Files you upload for processing: When you use editing tools (such as merge, reorder, remove pages, or repair), you may upload a PDF file. We process the file to produce the result you request.
  • Usage and device data: Like most websites, we may collect basic technical information such as browser type, pages visited, and approximate location derived from IP address. This helps us keep the service reliable, measure performance, and prevent abuse.
  • Cookies and similar technologies: We may use cookies for essential site functionality and analytics/advertising (where applicable). You can control cookies through your browser settings.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and operate the PDF tools you request (edit, merge, reorder, repair).
  • To maintain security, troubleshoot issues, and prevent fraud or abuse.
  • To understand how the site is used and improve performance and user experience.
  • To display ads where applicable (for example, to support free access to the service).

File Processing and Security

We aim to process documents securely and only for the purpose of delivering the output you request. Uploaded files may be temporarily stored in memory or on servers as needed for processing. We do not sell your document contents.

Please avoid uploading documents that contain highly sensitive personal information if you are not comfortable processing them online.

Sharing and Third Parties

We may use third-party services for analytics, advertising, and infrastructure. These providers may collect information through their own cookies or similar technologies. Their use of information is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Your Choices

  • You can manage cookies in your browser settings and clear stored site data at any time.
  • You may choose not to upload files; the site can still be browsed without submitting documents.
  • If you contact us, you can request that we delete your message history, subject to legal and operational needs.

Children’s Privacy

Repair PDF Ninja is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last updated” date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@repairpdf.ninja.
