About Repair PDF Ninja

Repair PDF Ninja is a free online PDF editor built to make everyday PDF tasks simple. You can edit, merge, reorder and repair PDF files directly from your browser—no installation and no account required.

What you can do

  • Edit PDFs online
  • Merge multiple PDFs into one
  • Reorder pages visually
  • Remove pages and repair common issues

How it works

Upload a PDF, choose a tool, preview your pages, and apply changes. When processing finishes, you can download the updated PDF instantly.

Security mindset

We aim to handle documents responsibly and limit processing to what’s needed to deliver your requested output. For more details, review our Privacy Policy.

Why it’s free

Our goal is to keep essential PDF tools accessible. The site may display advertisements to help cover hosting and development costs.

Responsible use

Please ensure you have the right to upload and modify any documents you process. If you are working with confidential or regulated information, consider whether online processing is appropriate for your situation.
